About

I am a creative, detail-oriented, software engineer with a proven track record of creating and implementing successful front and back end web applications. I am looking to bring my skills to a tech company that values public service and encourages growth among their employees.

Software Engineer & Full-Stack Web Developer

My professional career has granted me opportunities to work in a variety of environments, from Maryland and Colorado, to Alaska and Cambodia. The majority of this work has been in the public service sector, including non-profits, government agencies, and volunteer organizations. These positions allowed me to develop a diverse range of technical and interpersonal skillsets. With every new job, I quickly became focused on finding ways to optimize my workflow and minimize administrative tasks.

I learned how to use json files when creating data visualizations that showcased educational outcomes in Alaskan schools. I used JavaScript to automate case notes and data entry. I stored caseload data on over 100+ participants in MongoDB, allowing me to filter and sort them based on a variety of program metrics. These tools, which were originally intended to make my work life easier, developed into a passion. I created an app so I could view the NASA Picture of the Day. I developed a webpage to keep tabs on my friend’s food recommendations.

Learning to code has been one of the most difficult things I’ve ever done. Countless hours spent on Stack Overflow, Discord, and YouTube, trying to diagnose the error that is preventing my entire app from working. Hundreds of coding problems, thousands of Google searches, and at least a couple dozen of imposter syndrome moments. I came to realize that it was more important for me to be challenged in my work than to do something I thought was meaningful. Coding has struck the perfect balance, allowing me to work on projects that improve people’s lives while continuing to learn and be challenged every day.
